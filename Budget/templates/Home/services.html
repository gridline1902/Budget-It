Our Services
We provide the following features:

  • Ability to create multiple budgets: Users can create budgets for different months or categories, and access them anytime.
  • Automated calculation of the 50/30/20 rule: The website does the calculation automatically and displays the results in real-time.
  • Visualization of budget breakdown: The website displays a graphical representation of how the budget is divided into needs, wants, and savings.
  • Reminder notifications: Users can set reminders for important dates such as when bills are due or when savings goals should be met.
  • Currency options: Users can choose the currency in which they want their budget to be displayed.
  • User account: Users can create an account and access their budgets from any device, as long as they are logged in.
