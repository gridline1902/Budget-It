Welcome to our website!
We're here to help you stay on top of your finances and achieve your financial goals.

Our website calculates your monthly budget using the 50/30/20 rule, a popular and effective method for managing your money.
With this rule, you allocate 50% of your income to your needs (such as rent, groceries, and bills), 30% to your wants (such as dining out and entertainment), and 20% to your savings or debt payments.

This balanced approach ensures that you have enough for your essentials, while still allowing you to enjoy your life and build your wealth.
Our goal is to help you make smarter financial decisions and achieve your long-term goals. We believe that everyone can benefit from good money management, and we're here to make it easy and accessible for you.
Thank you for using our website, and we hope you enjoy our service!
